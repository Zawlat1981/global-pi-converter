Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Our app uses the Pi Network SDK to authenticate users and facilitate transactions within the Pi ecosystem. Any data processed through the SDK is handled according to Pi Network's official privacy standards.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with any third-party services. All currency conversion logic is performed within the application.

We prioritize the security of your interaction with our app. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our official developer channels in the Pi Network.
